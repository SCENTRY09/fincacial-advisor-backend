TITLE: PM Mudra Loan Scheme

CATEGORY: Government Scheme

SUMMARY: Mudra Loan provides unsecured business loans up to Rs. 10,00,000 for micro and small enterprises. No collateral required, making it accessible for entrepreneurs.

KEY POINTS:
- Loan amount: Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 10,00,000
- No collateral required
- No guarantee required
- Interest rate: 8-12% per annum
- Repayment period: 3-5 years
- Quick approval process

LOAN CATEGORIES:
- Shishu: Up to Rs. 50,000
- Kishore: Rs. 50,001 to Rs. 5,00,000
- Tarun: Rs. 5,00,001 to Rs. 10,00,000

ELIGIBILITY:
- Indian citizen
- Age 18+ years
- Self-employed or entrepreneur
- Business registered or unregistered
- No existing default
- Good credit history

ELIGIBLE BUSINESSES:
- Retail trade
- Manufacturing
- Services
- Agriculture
- Food processing
- Handicrafts
- Any micro/small business

BEST SUITED FOR:
- First-time entrepreneurs
- Self-employed professionals
- Small business owners
- Unorganized sector workers
- Women entrepreneurs
- Youth startups

RISK LEVEL: Moderate

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Prepare business plan
- Maintain proper documentation
- Use loan for business only
- Repay on time
- Maintain business records
- Avoid loan diversion

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Using loan for personal expenses
- Defaulting on repayment
- Not maintaining business records
- Borrowing beyond capacity
- Not comparing interest rates
- Ignoring loan terms

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme

APPLICATION PROCESS:
Approach bank or NBFC. Submit business plan and documents. Bank verifies and approves. Loan disbursed within 7-10 days. Repayment starts after 6-month moratorium.

INTEREST RATE COMPARISON:
Mudra Loan: 8-12% per annum
Personal loan: 10-18% per annum
Business loan: 9-15% per annum
Mudra offers competitive rates for business purpose.

REPAYMENT FLEXIBILITY:
Moratorium period available. EMI can be adjusted based on business cash flow. Prepayment without penalty. Refinancing options available.
