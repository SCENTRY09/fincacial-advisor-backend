TITLE: Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

CATEGORY: Government Scheme

SUMMARY: APY guarantees fixed monthly pension from age 60 onwards. Government co-contributes 50% of contribution for eligible subscribers, making it attractive for retirement planning.

KEY POINTS:
- Guaranteed pension from age 60
- Monthly pension: Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 5,000
- Government co-contribution: 50% of contribution
- Contribution: Rs. 42 to Rs. 210 monthly
- Enrollment age: 18-40 years
- Portable across India

PENSION AMOUNTS:
- Rs. 1,000 monthly: Rs. 84 contribution
- Rs. 2,000 monthly: Rs. 168 contribution
- Rs. 3,000 monthly: Rs. 252 contribution
- Rs. 4,000 monthly: Rs. 336 contribution
- Rs. 5,000 monthly: Rs. 420 contribution

GOVERNMENT CO-CONTRIBUTION:
- 50% of subscriber contribution
- Maximum Rs. 1,000 per year
- Available for 5 years from enrollment
- Only for eligible subscribers
- Automatic credit to account

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Self-employed individuals
- Unorganized sector workers
- Young professionals
- Retirement planners
- Low-income earners
- Informal sector workers

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Enroll between age 18-40
- Maintain regular contributions
- Provide Aadhaar and bank details
- Nominate beneficiary
- Continue contributions until age 60
- Verify enrollment confirmation

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Enrolling after age 40
- Missing contribution deadlines
- Not nominating beneficiary
- Withdrawing prematurely
- Not updating contact details
- Ignoring policy documents

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme

PENSION CALCULATION:
Contribution: Rs. 100 monthly from age 25
Government co-contribution: Rs. 100 monthly for 5 years
Total contribution: Rs. 1,00,000 (approximately)
Pension from age 60: Rs. 5,000 monthly (approximately)

SURVIVOR BENEFIT:
If subscriber dies before age 60, spouse can continue contributions and receive pension. If spouse doesn't continue, accumulated amount returned to nominee.

PORTABILITY:
APY account portable across India. Change location without affecting pension. Maintain single account throughout.
