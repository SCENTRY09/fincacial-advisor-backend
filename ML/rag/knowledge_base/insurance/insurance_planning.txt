TITLE: Comprehensive Insurance Planning

CATEGORY: Insurance

SUMMARY: Insurance planning ensures adequate protection against life, health, and accident risks. Proper planning provides financial security for family and dependents.

KEY POINTS:
- Life insurance: Income replacement
- Health insurance: Medical expense coverage
- Accident insurance: Injury protection
- Disability insurance: Income protection
- Property insurance: Asset protection
- Liability insurance: Legal protection

INSURANCE NEEDS ANALYSIS:
- Life insurance: 10-15x annual income
- Health insurance: Rs. 5,00,000-10,00,000
- Accident insurance: Rs. 2,00,000-5,00,000
- Disability insurance: 60% of annual income
- Property insurance: Full asset value

INSURANCE TYPES:
- Term insurance: Pure protection
- Endowment: Protection + savings
- Whole life: Lifetime protection
- Unit-linked: Protection + investment
- Health insurance: Medical coverage
- General insurance: Property/liability

BEST SUITED FOR:
- All individuals
- Families with dependents
- Business owners
- Professionals
- Homeowners
- Vehicle owners

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Assess insurance needs annually
- Buy insurance early
- Maintain continuous coverage
- Review policies regularly
- Increase coverage with inflation
- Nominate beneficiaries

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Insufficient coverage
- Overlapping policies
- Lapsing policies
- Not reviewing coverage
- Buying wrong insurance type
- Delaying claim submission

SOURCE TYPE: Financial Best Practice

INSURANCE LADDER APPROACH:
Age 25-35: Term insurance + health insurance
Age 35-50: Term insurance + health insurance + disability
Age 50+: Health insurance + critical illness + accident

PREMIUM OPTIMIZATION:
Buy term insurance early for lower premiums. Combine policies for discounts. Review annually for better rates. Maintain good health for lower premiums.

CLAIM DOCUMENTATION:
Keep policy documents safe. Maintain medical records. Report claims promptly. Provide complete documentation. Follow claim process carefully. Keep copies of submissions.
