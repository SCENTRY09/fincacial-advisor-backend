TITLE: PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)

CATEGORY: Government Scheme

SUMMARY: PMJJBY provides affordable life insurance coverage of Rs. 2,00,000 for accidental or natural death. Premium is only Rs. 436 per year, making it accessible to all citizens.

KEY POINTS:
- Coverage: Rs. 2,00,000
- Annual premium: Rs. 436 (Rs. 36.33 monthly)
- Age eligibility: 18-50 years
- Automatic enrollment through bank account
- Coverage for accidental and natural death
- No medical examination required

ELIGIBILITY:
- Indian citizen
- Age 18-50 years
- Active bank account
- Aadhaar linked to account
- No existing PMJJBY policy
- Good health status

COVERAGE DETAILS:
- Death due to accident: Rs. 2,00,000
- Death due to natural causes: Rs. 2,00,000
- Accidental disability: Rs. 1,00,000
- Partial disability: Rs. 50,000

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Young working professionals
- Low-income earners
- Self-employed individuals
- Gig economy workers
- Students
- Anyone seeking affordable coverage

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Enroll before June 30 each year
- Maintain active bank account
- Keep Aadhaar updated
- Inform bank of address changes
- Nominate beneficiary
- Verify enrollment confirmation

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Missing enrollment deadline
- Allowing bank account to become inactive
- Not nominating beneficiary
- Ignoring policy documents
- Assuming automatic renewal
- Not informing bank of changes

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme

CLAIM PROCESS:
Beneficiary submits claim form with death certificate to bank. Bank verifies and processes claim within 30 days. Amount credited to nominee's account.

COMPARISON:
PMJJBY: Rs. 436/year, Rs. 2,00,000 coverage
Private term insurance: Rs. 3,000-5,000/year, Rs. 50,00,000 coverage
PMJJBY suitable for basic coverage. Combine with term insurance for comprehensive protection.
