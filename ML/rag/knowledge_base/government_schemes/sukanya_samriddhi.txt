TITLE: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

CATEGORY: Government Scheme

SUMMARY: SSY is a savings scheme for girl child below 10 years. It offers 7.6% guaranteed returns with tax benefits, designed for education and marriage expenses.

KEY POINTS:
- For girl child below 10 years
- Guaranteed interest: 7.6% per annum
- Minimum annual deposit: Rs. 250
- Maximum annual deposit: Rs. 1,50,000
- Maturity period: 21 years
- Tax-free returns

ACCOUNT FEATURES:
- Opened at post office or authorized banks
- Account holder: Girl child (minor)
- Operated by: Parent/Guardian
- Deposits made in lump sum or installments
- Interest credited annually
- Can extend for 5-year blocks

DEPOSIT RULES:
- Deposit period: 15 years
- After 15 years: No deposits required
- Interest continues to compound
- Deposits can be made anytime during 15 years
- Minimum Rs. 250, maximum Rs. 1,50,000 annually

WITHDRAWAL RULES:
- Partial withdrawal: After 50% of tenure or age 10
- Full withdrawal: After 21 years
- For education: After age 18
- Premature closure: After 15 years only

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Parents of girl child
- Long-term savers
- Tax-conscious investors
- Education planners
- Marriage expense planners

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Open account before age 10
- Maintain minimum annual deposit
- Nominate beneficiary
- Keep account documents safe
- Update contact details
- Verify account status annually

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Opening account after age 10
- Missing annual deposit deadline
- Not nominating beneficiary
- Withdrawing prematurely
- Not maintaining documents
- Forgetting account details

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme

MATURITY CALCULATION:
Annual deposit: Rs. 1,00,000 for 15 years
Total invested: Rs. 15,00,000
Estimated maturity amount: Rs. 23,00,000 (approximately)

TAX BENEFITS:
Deduction under Section 80C: Rs. 1,50,000 per year
Interest earned: Tax-free
Maturity amount: Tax-free
No TDS on interest

EXTENSION BENEFIT:
After 21 years, extend for 5-year blocks. Interest continues at prevailing rate. Provides flexibility for long-term wealth accumulation.
