TITLE: PM Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)

CATEGORY: Government Scheme

SUMMARY: PMSBY provides accidental injury insurance coverage of Rs. 2,00,000 for just Rs. 12 per year. It covers accidental death and disability with minimal premium.

KEY POINTS:
- Coverage: Rs. 2,00,000 for accidental death
- Partial disability: Rs. 1,00,000
- Annual premium: Rs. 12 only
- Age eligibility: 18-70 years
- Automatic enrollment through bank account
- No medical examination required

COVERAGE DETAILS:
- Accidental death: Rs. 2,00,000
- Permanent total disability: Rs. 2,00,000
- Permanent partial disability: Rs. 1,00,000
- Covers all types of accidents
- 24/7 coverage

ELIGIBILITY:
- Indian citizen
- Age 18-70 years
- Active bank account
- Aadhaar linked to account
- No existing PMSBY policy
- Good health status

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Manual workers
- Construction workers
- Drivers
- Delivery personnel
- Outdoor workers
- Anyone with accident risk

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Enroll before May 31 each year
- Maintain active bank account
- Keep Aadhaar updated
- Nominate beneficiary
- Inform bank of address changes
- Verify enrollment confirmation

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Missing enrollment deadline
- Allowing bank account to become inactive
- Not nominating beneficiary
- Assuming automatic renewal
- Not updating contact details
- Ignoring policy documents

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme

CLAIM PROCESS:
Beneficiary submits claim form with accident report and medical documents to bank. Bank verifies and processes claim within 30 days. Amount credited to nominee's account.

COMPARISON WITH PMJJBY:
PMSBY: Accidental injury focus, Rs. 12/year
PMJJBY: Life insurance focus, Rs. 436/year
Both complement each other for comprehensive protection.

PREMIUM PAYMENT:
Automatic deduction from bank account on enrollment. Ensure sufficient balance on enrollment date. Premium remains Rs. 12 annually.
