TITLE: Public Provident Fund (PPF)

CATEGORY: Investment

SUMMARY: PPF is a government-backed savings scheme offering guaranteed returns with tax benefits. It provides financial security with 15-year maturity and flexible withdrawal options.

KEY POINTS:
- Guaranteed interest rate: 7.1% per annum (2024-25)
- Minimum annual deposit: Rs. 500
- Maximum annual deposit: Rs. 1,50,000
- Maturity period: 15 years
- Tax-free returns and interest
- Deductible under Section 80C

ACCOUNT FEATURES:
- Opened at post office or authorized banks
- Account number provided
- Deposits made in lump sum or installments
- Interest credited annually on March 31
- Can extend for 5-year blocks after maturity

WITHDRAWAL RULES:
- Partial withdrawal: After 7 years (50% of balance)
- Full withdrawal: After 15 years
- Premature closure: After 15 years only
- No withdrawal before 7 years

BEST SUITED FOR:
- Conservative investors
- Salaried employees
- Long-term savers
- Tax-conscious investors
- Retirement planning

RISK LEVEL: Low

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:
- Maintain minimum Rs. 500 annual deposit
- Deposit before March 31 each year
- Keep account active for full 15 years
- Nominate beneficiary
- Preserve account documents

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Missing annual deposit deadline
- Withdrawing prematurely
- Not utilizing full Rs. 1,50,000 limit
- Not nominating beneficiary
- Forgetting account details

SOURCE TYPE: Government Scheme
